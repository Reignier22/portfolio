About Me
Designing Is My Passion

Coding used to be something I detested. It's not something I'm good at out of enthusiasm for it. I've always been interested in the performing arts. That's why I took the Arts and Design Track in Senior High School. I had no idea what I was getting into when I started college. I didn't know how to use a computer either. Just because I'm not excellent at something doesn't mean I don't want to try it. That's why I decided to get a degree in information technology. When I was a freshman, I didn't know whether I would make it this far since I was ready to move to a different course. But look at me today. I can create a decent website, and I've also figured out the logic behind computer programming. If you're questioning your abilities right now, remember that the first step was always the hardest.
